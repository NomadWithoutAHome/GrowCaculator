What is Grow A Garden?

Grow A Garden is a massively popular farming simulation game on Roblox where players dive into the world of planting, growing, and harvesting crops to earn Sheckles, the game's virtual currency. At its peak, the game has attracted over 5 million concurrent players, thanks to its addictive and rewarding gameplay.

In Grow A Garden, your journey begins at Sam's Seed Shop, where you can buy seeds to start your garden. Using tools like sprinklers and taking advantage of weather events—such as the rare Blood Moon—you can increase the chances of growing mutated crops, which are key to maximizing your profits. Rare mutations like Shocked or Celestial can increase a crop's value by up to 150x, making them extremely valuable for trading and selling.

The game also features a unique gifting system that allows players to trade fruits, seeds, and gear with others. Understanding the true value of these items is essential to avoid scams and make smart, profitable trades in the community.

Whether you're a casual gardener or aiming to become a top trader, Grow A Garden offers deep strategy, a thriving economy, and endless farming fun—all within the vibrant world of Roblox.

The Value Calculation Formula

Step 1: Calculate Crop Value

Crop Value = Base Value × (Weight / Base Weight)²

Start by adjusting the crop's base value based on weight. Weight influences value quadratically, meaning heavier fruits increase in value significantly.

Step 2: Calculate Mutation Multiplier

Mutation Multiplier = Growth Mutation × (1 + ΣEnvironmental Mutations - Number of Environmental Mutations)

Apply both growth and environmental mutations. The multiplier increases with the sum of all mutation bonuses but is reduced by the number of different mutation types.

Step 3: Calculate Total Price

Total Price = Crop Value × Mutation Multiplier

Multiply the adjusted crop value by the mutation multiplier to get the final Sheckle value.

Parameters Explained

  • Base Value: The crop’s unique base Sheckle value
  • Weight: Actual crop weight in kg
  • Base Weight: Standard weight for each crop type
  • Growth Mutation: Golden (×20), Rainbow (×50)
  • ΣEnvironmental Mutations: Sum of all active environmental mutation bonuses
  • Number of Environmental Mutations: Total number of applied mutation types
  • Friend Boost (Optional): Up to +50% bonus value when playing with friends
